About Me

My career is driven by a passion for everything cryptocurrency related. After starting my career in TradFi trading at RBC, I transitioned fully into crypto, applying my analytical skills at top-tier firms including BitMEX, Paradigm, and Chainalysis. My work has spanned from building AML monitoring systems and fine-tuning ML models for trading products to investigating on-chain forensic cases for government agencies.

My passion for crypto began at Carnegie Mellon, where I took every available class on the subject, attended conferences, and joined the university's blockchain club. Today, I'm building on that foundation as an MSc candidate in Information Security at UCL, where I'm focusing on cryptography and conducting research. I'm eager to contribute to a team at the frontier of the crypto, tackling complex technical challenges and advancing cutting-edge research.
